Ninja Blog System

Ninja Blog System gives users the opportunity to create their own blog.

In the blog users can post their opinion on a particular subject, use it as a personal online diary or for advertisement.

Ninja Blog System allows users to:

  • Post text and images
  • Present links to other blogs and web pages
  • Leave comments on their or other users' posts
  • See all the registered users in the Ninja Blog System
  • See the newest post and etc.
